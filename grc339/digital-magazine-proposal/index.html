Introduction

Stinky Lunch Club

This is a space where your lunchbox isn't a just a meal, but a revolution. Where the stories you share over family dinners are shared with the world. Here we can forget about the stinky lunch haters and discover our individual identity and our definition of what it means to be Asian American. We'll explore the groundworks of our cultures, from students taking a modern spin on the traditional Tinikling dance to the debates on the best boba tea flavor. We'll unpack all your favorite Asian artists and Asian American start ups and small businesses.
